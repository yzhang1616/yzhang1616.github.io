Euler Characteristic Method for the Largest Eigenvalue of a Random Matrix

The material on this webpage accompanies the article Euler Characteristic Method for the Largest Eigenvalue of a Random Matrix by Satoshi Kuriki, Nobuki Takayama and Yi Zhang.

Example1_HGM.nb, a Mathematica notebook for the demonstration of the holonomic gradient method for the evaluation of expection of an Euler characteristic number. It is based on joint work with Satoshi Kuriki and Nobuki Takayama. The notebook requires the availability of Koutschan's package HolonomicFunctions.m.
