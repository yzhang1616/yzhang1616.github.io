On D-finiteness of a symmetric function

The material on this webpage accompanies the article On D-finiteness of a symmetric function by Bruce W. Westbury and Yi Zhang.

Fake_Degree_Sequence.nb, a Mathematica notebook for computing a linear q-difference equation satisfied by the fake degree sequence associated to a given representation and a given simple complex Lie algebra by the method of creative telescoping and the closure properties of the class of q-holonomic sequences. The notebook requires the availability of Koutschan's package HolonomicFunctions.m.
