Set partitions and G2 webs

The material on this webpage accompanies the article Set partitions and G2 webs by Jordan Tirrell, Bruce W. Westbury and Yi Zhang.

Mihailovs_Conjecture.nb, a Mathematica notebook for proving Mihailovs' conjecure by the method of creative telescoping. The notebook requires the availability of Koutschan's package HolonomicFunctions.m.
